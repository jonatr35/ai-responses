# Shin Nerve Recovery Exercises

## 1. Nerve Gliding Exercise

**Starting Position:** Sit with your affected leg extended in front of you

**Steps:**
- Point your toes down toward the floor - hold 2-3 seconds
- Flex your toes up toward your shin - hold 2-3 seconds
- Repeat 10-15 times
- Add gentle ankle circles (5 each direction) as progression

**Frequency:** 1-2 times daily

---

## 2. Calf Stretch (Gastrocnemius)

**Instructions:**
- Stand facing a wall, hands on wall at shoulder height
- Step one foot back, keeping that leg straight
- Keep back heel flat on ground
- Bend front knee and lean forward until you feel stretch in back calf
- Hold 30 seconds, repeat 2-3 times per leg

**Soleus Variation:** Same position but bend the back knee slightly while keeping heel down

---

## 3. Anterior Shin Stretch

**Instructions:**
- Kneel on a soft surface
- Sit back gently onto your heels with tops of feet flat on floor
- Feel stretch along front of shins and ankles
- Hold 30 seconds, repeat 2-3 times

**Standing Alternative:** Point one foot behind you, press top of foot into ground, hold chair for balance

---

## 4. Hip Flexor Stretch

**Instructions:**
- Start in lunge position with right foot forward
- Lower left knee to ground (use cushion)
- Keep torso upright, gently push hips forward
- Feel stretch in front of back hip
- Hold 30 seconds, repeat 2-3 times per side

**Tip:** Engage core, avoid arching lower back
